Welcome

This site will help you solve your Rubiks cube!

This site was built for the final project of CS50x.

This site will help you solve a Rubiks cube. After entering your current cube configuration, the site will calculate what your next moves should be, all the way through to solving the cube!

There are different ways to solve a Rubiks cube, the manual method on this site uses an 8-step method as described in the adjacent video from Wired. If you want to understand why each move is being made, or learn to solve a cube without this site, this video will explain. If you just want to get cracking, no need to watch the video!
